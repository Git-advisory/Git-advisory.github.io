Industries

GITA provides services across various industries, such as technology, automotive, and manufacturing. Our expertise drives innovative solutions that enhance efficiency, empower businesses to overcome challenges, and help achieve their goals while maximizing profitability.

Engineering and Technology

The Industry 4.0 and Industry 5.0 would replace the existing industries with IOT based technologies using sensors, internet connectivity, autonomous, stealth, digitization and sustainability. This includes Train Transportation, Industrial Drones, Medical Drones, Furnaces through Additive Manufacturing etc. As the technologies evolve, there is a need for technology transfer, exchange of Innovations and new markets. GITA helps you explore and exploit the ground breaking Innovations for commercial purposes..

EV and Automobile Industries

The Electric Vehicle and the Automotive Industries are going through a Generation Transformation from the Internal Combustion Engines to Electric Engines, Internet Connectivity and Driverless Technologies. Understand the industry better through us by connecting with the Innovative German Startups in Electric Motorcycles, Electric Cars, Battery Manufacturers and Other OEM Components. GITA supports your business in Joint Ventures, Internationalisation, Purchase, Mergers, Acquisitions, Strategic Alliance android Localised Manufacturing.
